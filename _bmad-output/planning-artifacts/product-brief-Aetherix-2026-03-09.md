# Product Brief: Aetherix

<!-- Content will be appended sequentially through collaborative workflow steps -->

## Executive Summary

Aetherix is an Agentic AI solution designed to solve the notoriously difficult, manual, and stressful process of F&B forecasting for hotel and restaurant managers. By moving away from traditional "pull" dashboards that require manual data correlation, Aetherix operates as a proactive "push" agent. It combines internal operational data with external semantic context (weather, events) to provide easy, intuitive access to reliable staffing and prep information. The goal is to reduce manager stress, enable better-informed decision-making, drive financial savings (reducing food waste and optimizing labor), and ultimately improve both the employee and customer experience.

---

## Core Vision

### Problem Statement

F&B forecasting is notoriously difficult, manual, and stressful. Hotel and restaurant managers currently rely on a mix of gut feeling, manual data gathering (like checking weather apps), and disparate tools (like PMS systems) to predict demand and schedule staff.

### Problem Impact

Inaccurate forecasting creates cascading operational failures:
- **Financial Loss:** Overstaffing blows labor budgets, while inaccurate prep leads to food waste.
- **Experience Degradation:** Understaffing leads to a bad employee experience (burnout, stress) which directly degrades the overall customer experience.
- **Time Sync:** Managers lose valuable time wrestling with data instead of focusing on customer care.

### Why Existing Solutions Fall Short

Existing solutions (like Mews, Guac, Kikleo, etc.) primarily operate on a "pull" model. They act as data repositories or static dashboards that require managers to log in, unify external semantic context (weather, local events) with internal data manually, and interpret the results. They lack the proactive "push" of unified, contextualized insights. *(Note: Deeper competitive research is pending.)*

### Proposed Solution

Aetherix is a proactive ambient agent, not just a dashboard. It acts as a human-in-the-loop copilot that unifies internal PMS records with an external semantic layer (events, weather). By using Agentic AI, it contextualizes the data and "pushes" reliable, easy-to-understand insights to the manager, freeing them from manual spreadsheet correlations.

## Target Users

### Primary Users: Departmental Managers (F&B / Front Office / Housekeeping)

**Context & Motivations:**
The operational backbone of the hotel. They are responsible for converting occupancy forecasts into daily reality, ensuring quality service while strictly managing labor costs and staff well-being. They live in a high-pressure environment where understaffing leads to guest complaints and overstaffing leads to reprimands from finance.

**Problem Experience:**
Their current scheduling process is "semi-analytical but largely manual." They receive an occupancy forecast from the Revenue Manager, look at historical ratios (e.g., 1 server per 20-30 covers), build an Excel or Planday schedule, and then scramble to adjust it daily based on last-minute cancellations, sickness, or unexpected local events. They spend hours wrestling with data instead of managing their teams or guests.

**Success Vision:**
Success is waking up to an ambient, proactive notification that says: *"Occupancy dropped by 10% for tomorrow evening, but a local concert just announced means F&B foot traffic will likely spike. Recommend maintaining current F&B staffing but cutting one Housekeeping shift. Approve?"* They want their time back and the anxiety of "getting it wrong" removed.

### Secondary Users: Director of Operations / General Manager

**Context & Motivations:**
They oversee the entire property's profitability and guest satisfaction scores. They don't build the schedules, but they hold the Department Managers accountable for the labor-to-revenue ratios and the P&L.

**Problem Experience:**
They lack real-time visibility into *why* a manager overstaffed a Tuesday. They only see the financial impact at the end of the month during P&L reviews, which is too late to fix it.

**Success Vision:**
A macro-level dashboard (or weekly AI-generated summary) showing how accurately each department is forecasting, the cost savings generated by Aetherix's recommendations, and an audit trail of *why* scheduling decisions were made.

### User Journey (The "Breaking Point" to "Aha!" Moment)

- **The Breaking Point:** It's Thursday afternoon. The F&B manager has spent 3 hours building next week's schedule in Excel based on a 70% occupancy forecast. On Friday morning, a massive local event is announced, and group bookings surge to 95%. The manager has to throw out the schedule, scramble to call in off-duty staff, deal with frustrated employees, and face angry guests due to slow service. The manual process has officially failed them under dynamic conditions.
- **Discovery & Onboarding:** The Director of Operations mandates Aetherix. The system connects to their existing PMS (Mews) and scheduling software in minutes. The manager doesn't need to learn a complex new dashboard; they just need to provide their WhatsApp number or Slack handle handle.

## Success Metrics

### User Success

*For Department Managers:*
- Simplified, stress-free planning process (Time spent creating schedules reduced significantly).
- Gain reliable, contextualized information proactively before peaks of activity.
- Zero surprises regarding understaffed shifts or over-budget labor costs.

*For the Director of Operations:*
- Clear visibility into operational performance across departments.
- Coordination built on reliable, shared forecast data rather than disparate departmental Excel sheets.

### Business Objectives

- **Short-Term (3 Months):** Successful integration within a pilot hotel's IT stack. The primary indicator of adoption success will be an **increase in staff satisfaction** (less burnout from understaffing, more predictable schedules).
- **Long-Term (12 Months):** Achieving product-market fit demonstrated by **speed of deployment** across new properties and high **referral rates** from the initial pilot hotels.

### Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

These metrics will determine if the Agent is functioning correctly and delivering ROI:

**Data & Prediction Accuracy:**
- **Forecast Accuracy (MAPE):** Occupancy, F&B covers, and Staffing requirement prediction errors must be consistently `< 10–15%`.

**Product Adoption & Operational Trust:**
- **Staffing decision accuracy:** % of staffing decisions aligned with the AI agent's recommendation.
- **Agent Influence:** % of agent recommendations actively followed/approved by the manager.
- **Proactive Engagement:** % of managers consulting the forecast before planning (shifting from reactive to proactive).

**Financial & Service Impact:**
- **Labor Cost Variance:** Actual vs Expected labor costs reduced by `-3% to -8%`.
- **Idle Labor Hours:** Reduction in paid hours during unpredicted quiet periods.

## MVP Scope

### Core Features

The MVP will focus strictly on delivering the core value of proactive, contextualized forecasting without the overhead of complex interfaces:
- **1-Way PMS Integration:** Read-only connection to Mews/Apaleo to retrieve historical and current occupancy/reservations.
- **Semantic Data Engine:** Ingestion of local events (PredictHQ) and weather to provide context to the baseline forecast.
- **Agentic Push Notifications:** Delivery of insights via SMS/WhatsApp or email directly to the manager. 
- **Explainable Predictions:** Every recommendation pushed to the manager will include the "Why" (e.g., "Predicting +20 covers due to 80% occupancy combined with severe rain driving guests to stay dining in-house").

### Out of Scope for MVP

To ensure speed to market and validate the core hypothesis of "push" vs "pull", the following are explicitly excluded:
- **Complex Customizable Dashboards:** No massive Streamlit or React web apps for data visualization. (Note on EU regulations: While explainability is mandated by the AI Act, this can be achieved through clear text reasoning in the notification itself or an append-only log, rather than a full exploratory dashboard).
- **2-Way PMS Write Sync:** No automatic schedule adjustments or writing data back to the PMS/scheduling software.
- **Full Autonomous Action:** Action remains strictly Human-in-the-Loop (the agent advises, the manager executes).

### MVP Success Criteria

- Successful processing of a PMS webhook trigger into a contextualized SMS/WhatsApp notification.
- A pilot manager successfully acting upon an Aetherix recommendation to adjust a shift.
- A verified positive variance in the MAPE forecast accuracy vs the baseline PMS forecast.

### Future Vision (2-3 Years)

Aetherix evolves into the ultimate, seamless hospitality operations brain:
- **Omniscient Context:** Incorporating real-time social sentiment, neighborhood traffic, and group pricing data.
- **Hyper-Personalized Communication:** The agent knows *how* and *when* to speak to different personas. It texts the F&B manager about covers at 3 PM, emails the GM a P&L summary on Friday, and alerts the Housekeeping manager about early check-ins at 7 AM.
- **Autonomous Orchestration:** Evolving from "Human-in-the-Loop" to "Human-on-the-Loop", where Aetherix can autonomously message temp-worker pools to cover shifts if predictive demand surges unexpectedly.
- **Zero-Friction Integration:** A standardized API architecture that makes integrating Aetherix into any hotel's existing stack (legacy or modern) plug-and-play.
